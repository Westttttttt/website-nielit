WELCOME TO NIELIT IMPHAL OFFICIAL WEBSITE        NOTICE

The National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT) Imphal is a premier institute established to promote and develop quality education and training in the field of Information, Electronics, and Communication Technology (IECT). It operates under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India.

Located in the heart of Imphal, the institute has been contributing significantly to the development of skilled professionals in Manipur and the North East region. NIELIT Imphal provides various long-term and short-term courses in IT, electronics, and computer science, aiming to empower the youth with technical knowledge and practical skills.

NIELIT Imphal fosters an environment of innovation, technical excellence, and continuous learning. The institute is equipped with modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and a focus on industry-relevant training. Through its commitment to high standards, it aims to prepare students to meet the global demands of the IT industry.

The vision of NIELIT Imphal is to be the most admired institution for providing quality education and training in the field of IECT, thereby contributing to the Digital India initiative and national development.

ACCREDITATION

National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Bangalore accredited and awarded B+ grade on 10th February 2007. It is the first college in Manipur which is ever assessed by the NAAC before completion of 4th year of its inauguration. Further, NAAC assessed the College for 2nd Cycle of Accreditation and awarded B Grade on June 25, 2015 .
